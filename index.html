Responsive Web Design

Importance of Responsive Web Design, the problems it solves and why you need it.

The average consumer nowadays uses their mobile device to access websites more frequently than they do on their desktop. It is the fact that ease of use and mobility make it easier for consumers to view and shop websites on their mobile devices now more than ever. This is leading to a growing number of companies that want to offer the same desktop experience to mobile users. Responsive web design brings consumers the same user experience they would have on their desktop on their mobile device. A lack of cohesion between mobile devices and desktop can lead to a poor user experience that can damage a company's digital image. Web developers are able to implement one design that can be viewed seamlessly on all devices with responsive web design. It is easier and cost effective for designers to maintain one design across multiple platforms rather than to adapt a design concept for each device.
